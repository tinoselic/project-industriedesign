KUKA KR 800 R3200-2 PA


Heavy Duty Palletizing Robot


The KR 800 R3200-2 PA stands for unbeatable and unbridled power in a compact design.

The heavy-duty palletizing robot KUKA KR 800 R3200-2 PA was developed for precise handling in the shortest cycle times and can move workpieces with payloads of up to 800 kg/ 1764 lb with a range of up to 3200 mm/ 126 inches. The extremely torsion-resistant design combined with powerful drives enables precise and quick handling of heavy, large-format components with high moments of inertia. The palletizer offers maximum performance in the smallest space and is equipped for a wide range of applications, from battery handling to mega and giga casting.

Its appearance unmistakably shows the concentrated energy, speed and performance. It looks expressive, extremely agile and, despite his enormous physical characteristics, radiates the light-footedness of a wiry athlete. Organically designed, powerful components give the palletizer a lively appearance and show the muscles with which the robot achieves peak performance. Flowing shape transitions withstand the enormous flow of force and the extreme physical dynamic properties.
