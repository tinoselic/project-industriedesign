KUKA KR 470 R3200-2 PA

Heavy Duty Palletizing Robot



The palletizing robot KUKA KR 470 R3200-2 PA was developed for precise handling in the shortest cycle times and can move workpieces with payloads of up to 470 kg/ 1036 lbs with a range of up to 3200 mm/ 126 inches. It offers maximum performance in the smallest space and is equipped for the latest applications, from battery handling to mega- and giga-casting. The extremely torsion-resistant design combined with powerful drives enables precise and quick handling of heavy, large-format components with high moments of inertia.

The KR 470 R3200-2 PA stands for unbeatable and unbridled power in a compact format.

Its appearance unmistakably shows the concentrated energy, speed and performance. It looks expressive, incredibly powerful, extremely agile and, despite his enormous physical characteristics, radiates the light-footedness of a wiry athlete. Organically designed, powerful components give the machine a lively appearance and show the muscles with which the robot achieves top performance. The second-life-capable palletizer offers a long service life and, thanks to its low energy consumption, a climate-friendly overall package.
